I work on computer vision, machine learning, robotics, and artificial intelligence. My goal is to build a machine that is at least as intelligent as human beings. I believe that simplicity is the ultimate sophistication. Such a machine can only be realized by following the principle. I am currently with the Mitsubishi Electric Research Laboratories (MERL), Cambridge, MA, USA. I have several intern position openings. I am looking forward to work with people that share the same passion. A copy of my resume can be found in link.
