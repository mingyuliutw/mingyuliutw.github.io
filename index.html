I do fundamental and applied computer vision and deep learning research at Mitsubishi Electric Research Labs (MERL) located at Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA. I am particularly interested in deep unsupervised learning for image understanding. I believe simplicity is the ultimate sophistication and a truly intelligent machine can only be realized by following this principle. A copy of my resume can be found in link.
